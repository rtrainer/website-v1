A Big THANK YOU to our sponsors!

Without the generosity of our sponsors and donors, we would not have the opportunity to provide enriching STEM experiences to students all across Howard County Public Schools (HCPSS). By sponsoring Team 3748 Ragnarok Robotics, you not only help a local team succeed, but also support the next generation of inventors, problem-solvers, and engineers. Your donation helps in purchasing robotics parts and tools, registering for competitions, and funding outreach programs.

Initially focused on local organizations such as the Boys and Girls Clubs, Food Share, and the Rescue Mission, the foundation recognized a growing need for skilled manufacturing employees globally. AFCEA’s founders, a group of communicators led by David Sarnoff, experienced firsthand how open dialogue and strong relationships between government and industry in times of peace can help ensure effective communications during times of war. Sarnoff, who became president of RCA, had served as a brigadier general in World War II. In 1946, AFCEA was formed from the U.S. Veterans Signal Association and the American Signal Corps Association to promote communication, dialogue, and open and ethical exchanges of information between the public and private sectors. As the association’s outreach has broadened, this goal remains the pillar of AFCEA International.
